Welcome to Rolls-Royce History

Rolls-Royce, established in 1906 by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce, is synonymous with luxury and innovation. Below, you can explore the history of the founders and the timeline of all the cars created by Rolls-Royce, accompanied by spaces for their images.

Charles Rolls

Charles Stewart Rolls (1877–1910) was a British motoring and aviation pioneer. He co-founded Rolls-Royce in 1906, focusing on car sales and business development.

Henry Royce

Sir Frederick Henry Royce (1863–1933) was a mechanical engineer and the principal designer of Rolls-Royce cars. His pursuit of engineering excellence defined the company's ethos.
